The object of Virus-sweeper is to clear the board without landing on any viruses in the quickest amount of time. Viruses are scattered around the board and clicking on one will give your computer a virus :(

Clicking on a square will reveal whether it is a virus or a computer and if you click on a virus you lose. If you click on a computer it gives you a number which represents the amount of viruses in bordering squares. For example, if there is a computer with a red "2", two of its bordering squares have a virus.
